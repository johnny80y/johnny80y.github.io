What is this tool for?

Depending on the information you have collected, this tool provides you with a collection of websites that allow you to further your investigation.
Just click on the piece of information you would like to use to start your investigation.
You can always come back to the Main Page if you discover an additional piece of intel and need a different set of tools.

Note that you can use the forward/backward/reload-buttons in your browser to navigate this tool.
